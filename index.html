Strategic Direction — Pre-filled from Intake

Preliminary Strategic Direction — Please Review

Overall Website Goal

Kiso Group needs a more elevated, credible, and visually compelling website that better reflects the quality, sophistication, and scale of the work the company is already doing. The current website has useful content and photography, but the online presence does not yet fully match the caliber of the company’s work or the expectations of high-value prospects, referral partners, and decision-makers. The new website should help Kiso Group compete more effectively in situations where competitors may not be doing better work, but may appear more polished online.

Strategic Positioning

Kiso Group should be positioned as a premium aquatic construction and construction partner with deep technical expertise, strong execution capability, and high-end project experience.

“Luxury aquatic construction backed by disciplined construction expertise.”

Alternative direction to refine: “High-end aquatic environments built with precision, experience, and construction intelligence.”

The brand should not feel like a trendy pool company or a flashy social-first contractor. It should feel established, capable, premium, technically strong, and visually refined.

Desired Brand Perception

After visiting the website, prospects should feel that Kiso Group is highly capable, experienced, trustworthy, premium, organized, technically knowledgeable, able to handle complex work, and more sophisticated than a standard pool contractor — backed by real project proof, not just marketing language. The site should communicate that Kiso Group does serious work for serious projects without feeling cold, corporate, or overdone.

Visual Direction

Elevated, clean, editorial, and project-led — more architectural or design publication than contractor brochure. References discussed: editorial, high-end, architectural, clean, refined, portfolio-driven, image-forward, premium Miami / South Florida luxury, Restoration Hardware visual sensibility. Photography should do a lot of the heavy lifting; the new website should allow projects to speak for themselves.

Tone

Premium but approachable — technical but not dry, confident but not flashy, sophisticated but clear, editorial rather than promotional.

Portfolio / Project Strategy

The portfolio should be a major component of the website. Rather than a basic gallery, selected projects should be presented as individual portfolio pages using a consistent template. Direction: prioritize the strongest and most relevant work, use the best 3–4 images per featured project, and avoid overloading the site with weaker or outdated projects. Focus on work that reflects the type and quality Kiso Group wants to attract moving forward.

Logo / Brand Treatment

The red element of the current logo should remain part of the brand identity. Typography and font treatment may be refined or modernized, but the red logo mark/detail should be preserved. Original logo files are still needed.

Priority Audience

High-value residential, luxury, commercial, and referral-driven prospects who need to feel confident Kiso Group can execute complex aquatic construction. Potential audiences: luxury homeowners, developers, general contractors, architects/designers, commercial property owners, referral partners, and high-end residential decision-makers.

Competitive / Inspiration Direction

WW Gay and Power Design were discussed as inspiration/reference companies that communicate scale, credibility, culture, and operational strength well — not as direct competitors. The takeaway is how established companies use their websites to communicate capability, process, scale, culture, and professional maturity, translated into a more boutique, premium, aquatic/construction-specific expression.

SEO Direction

Strong foundational SEO including page titles, meta descriptions, alt tags, structured headings, and structured data where applicable — supporting both traditional search and AI-driven discovery. Priority themes likely include South Florida aquatic construction, luxury pool construction, custom pool builder, high-end pool contractor, and Miami / South Florida service areas.
